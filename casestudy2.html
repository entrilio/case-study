Our Comprehensive Solution

We implemented a holistic security program covering devices, identity, culture, and compliance.

Device Management

  • Deployed Kandji (Mac) & Intune (Windows)
  • Enforced encryption & OS updates
  • Automated new hire device provisioning

Identity & Access

  • Centralized SSO with Okta & Google
  • Established role-based access control
  • Automated deprovisioning workflows

Security Posture

  • Implemented 1Password for Teams & 2FA
  • Audited all app permissions & API access
  • Enabled Workspace audit logs & alerting

Culture & Compliance

  • Introduced security awareness training
  • Delivered clean policy documentation
  • Provided quarterly health checks

The Results: A Secure, Scalable Foundation

The startup is now fully managed, compliant, and ready for future growth and certification.

Total Device Compliance

From zero oversight to complete management, every device is now secure and compliant.

Offboarding Efficiency Gains

Manual, risky offboarding that took hours is now fully automated and complete in minutes.

Ready for SOC 2 and Beyond

With a robust security foundation, the team can now pursue SOC 2 compliance, confident that no data is lost or left in dormant SaaS apps post-departure.
